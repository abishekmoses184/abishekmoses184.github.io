Photography Services

From golden-hour mobile shots to DSLR studio setups — I capture moments that last forever, then refine them with professional editing tools and creative intuition.

Overview

What this service covers

Capturing Life,
One Frame at a Time

My photography journey started with mobile photography, where I developed an eye for composition, lighting, and timing. I'm currently expanding into DSLR and mirrorless systems to bring even higher quality to every project.

Whether it's portraiture, street photography, event coverage, or creative concept shoots, I approach every session with the same dedication — understanding the subject, finding the perfect light, and telling a visual story.

Post-processing is an integral part of my workflow. I use Adobe Photoshop and PicsArt on both PC and mobile, and increasingly leverage AI-powered tools to achieve results that are polished, vivid, and true to the intended mood.

My Process

How I work
01

Brief & Concept

I start with a conversation — understanding the purpose, audience, mood, and any specific requirements. This shapes the entire shoot strategy.

02

Scouting & Setup

Location matters. I scout the best spots, assess natural light conditions, and set up accordingly to make the most of the environment.

03

The Shoot

Capturing a variety of angles, compositions, and moments — ensuring there's plenty of choice during selection and editing.

04

Editing & Delivery

Colour grading, retouching, and export in the required format and resolution. Final delivery via Google Drive or direct transfer.
