UI / UX Design

Creating digital experiences that are beautiful to look at and effortless to use — through research, wireframing, prototyping, and pixel-perfect visual design.

Overview

What this service covers

Design That Puts People First

Good UI/UX design isn't just about how something looks — it's about how it works, how it feels, and how quickly a user can accomplish their goal. I approach every design project with empathy for the end user and a keen attention to visual detail.

My design process spans from conceptual wireframing and user flow mapping through to high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes. I primarily work in Canva for quick, polished deliverables and am continually building skills in dedicated UX tools.

Accessibility, visual hierarchy, and consistent design systems are core principles in my workflow — ensuring designs that are both beautiful and universally usable.

Design Principles

How I approach every project
01

User-Centred

Every decision is grounded in understanding user needs, goals, and pain points. Design serves the user, not the other way around.

02

Clarity First

Clear visual hierarchy, readable typography, and intuitive navigation — reducing friction at every step of the user journey.

03

Consistent Systems

Using reusable components, consistent spacing, and a unified colour palette to create cohesive, scalable design systems.

04

Accessible Design

Designing with contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, and inclusive patterns to ensure everyone can use the product.

Design Process

From brief to final handoff
01

Research & Discovery

Understanding the target users, business objectives, competitor landscape, and any existing brand guidelines or constraints.

02

Information Architecture

Mapping out navigation structures, user flows, and content hierarchy before a single pixel is placed.

03

Wireframing

Low-fidelity sketches and digital wireframes to rapidly explore layout options and get early feedback.

04

High-Fidelity Mockups

Full-colour, pixel-perfect designs with typography, iconography, imagery, and micro-interaction details.

05

Prototype & Handoff

Interactive prototypes for user testing, followed by developer-ready assets and design specifications.
